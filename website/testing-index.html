Dealing With Exam Stress

By Majh

  • Twitter
  • Facebook
  • Tumblr

Keep your work organised
Perhaps use post-it notes?

It's important to ensure all your work is kept organised and easy for you to use. This helps reduce your stress! You could even stick things, like little notes, to your wall or desk!

Getting enough sleep
8-10 hours EVERY NIGHT

It's important to sleep at least 8-10 hours every single night. Sleeping is better than staying up and studying all night!

Keep your health up
Loads of water and food

Nourish your body with lots of healthy grub. Fuel your body and mind with lots of greens, fruit, nuts and seeds for healthy energy boosting snacks and make sure you have a balanced diet of three meals a day. The NHS states that "the European Food Safety Authority recommends that women should drink about 1.6 litres of fluid and men should drink about 2.0 litres of fluid per day".

Talking to others help
Friends, Parents and Teachers

It is easier to talk to others about what you're stressing over and you'll find that it will somehow lift the stress from your shoulders.

Believe in yourself and work harder!
